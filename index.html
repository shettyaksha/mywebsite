WELCOME TO MY PRESENTATION

MY VISIT AND EXPERIENCE IN HYDERABAD

OUR VISIT TO HYDERABAD WAS OF 3 TO 4 DAYS . OUR FIRST VISIT WAS TO THE 'RAMOJI FILM CITY' . THE VISIT HAD BEEN THE TIME OF MY LIFE WHICH I SPENT WITH MY COUSINS AND FAMILY . WE WENT ON MANY RIDES,LEARNT HOW THINGS WORK AT BOLLYWOOD AND OTHER THINGS TOO. THERE WERE BUILDINGDS EVERYWHERE AND THEY SHOWED US HOW THEY CREATE FALSE HOUSES AND VILLAGES FOR SHOOTINGS. WE VISITED A MAGIC CUM GHOST HOUSE WHICH WAS REALLY HILARIOUS AND SCARY AS WELL. THERE WAS A NEW BIRD PARK IN RAMOJI FILM CITY . IT IS THE NEW SANCTUARY TO HUNDREDS OF EXOTIC BIRDS,A FREE ZONE AREA, AND A SPECIAL ZONE WHICH HOSTS ONLY OSTRICHES. THIS PARK HOLDS A WIDE RANGE OF SPECIES LIKE THE CAROLINA DUCK,EMPEROR GOOSE, SCARLET IBIS, WHITE STORK, CHILEAN FLAMINGO,VICTORIA CROWNED PIGEON ETC . AT NIGHT WE STAYED AT GOLDEN LOTUS HOTEL . THE NEXT DAY WE WENT TO NEHRU ZOOLOGICAL PARK . IT IS A MUST SEE FOR NATURE AND WILD LIFE LOVERS . IT ALSO HAS RARE SPECIES ANIMALS AND BIRDS, MOST OF WHICH ARE HOUSES ON CONDITIONS RESEMBLING THEIR NATURAL HABITATS AS MUCH AS POSSIBLE . THIS HOLDS THE DISTINCTION AS A FIRST ZOO TO CREATE MOATED ENCLOSURES FOR VARIOUS ANIMALS. WE WENT TO SEE THE HYDERABAD'S ICONIC HUSSAIN SAGAR LAKE,SITUATED IN THE HEART OF THE CITY . ADJACENT TO THE LAKE, LIE THE LUMBINI PARK AND BIRLA MANDIR . IN THE 3RD DAY WE WENT TO SNOW WORLD . IT IS AN AMUSEMENT PARK IN HYDERABAD . BEFORE ENTERING , THEY PROVIDED US WARM JACKETS, GLOVES AND WATER-PROOF SHOES TO KEEP US WARM . THERE IS A SNOW PLAY AREA,A SNOW SLIDE,SNOW VALLEYS AND MOUNTAINS,ICE- BOARDING,TOBOGGAN RIDES . TEA STALLS ARE THERE TO ENJOY SIP OF HOT TEA OR COFFEE SITTING ON SNOW CHAIRS. IN THE EVENING OF THE LAST DAY WE VISITED CHARMINAR . IT IS A POPULAR TOURIST PLACE IN HYDERBAD THAT WAS CONSTRUCTED BY SULTAN MOHAMMAD QULI QUTUB SHAH TO HONOR HIS WIFE,BHAGMATI . I ENJOYED MY TRIP TO THE CHARMINAR VERY MUCH . IT WAS REALLY AN UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCE AND GAVE US A GLIMPSE OF OUR COUNTRY'S PROUD HISTORY. A TRIP TO HYDERABAD IS INCOMPLETE WITHOUT TASTING THE FAMOUS HYDREABADI BIRYANI OR HYDREABADI DUM BIRYANI .IT IS FAMOUS BECAUSE OF ITS ABILITY TO IMBIBE MANY FOOD INGREDIENTS. WE WENT TO A HOTEL TO TASTE THE HYDERABADI BIRYANI . THE FOOD WAS TASTY AND DELICIOUS . AFTER SOMETIME WE HEADED BACK TO THE HOTEL THAT WE STAYED AT, THEN WE PACKED OUR BAGS AND RETURNED BACK. EVERYONE WERE SO DELIGHTED THAT THEY VISITED HYDERBAD. I ENJOYED MY TRIP TO HYDERABAD. I ALWAYS CHERISH MY VISIT TO HYDERABAD AND I RECOMMEND YOU TO VISIT IT ONCE IN YOUR LIFETIME.  

CHARMINAR

NEHRU ZOOLOGICAL PARK
